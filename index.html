Manali

Manali is a popular hill station in Himachal Pradesh, India, known for its breathtaking landscapes, snow-capped mountains, and adventure activities. Situated in the Kullu Valley along the Beas River, it attracts tourists year-round for skiing, trekking, river rafting, and paragliding. Manali is also a gateway to the scenic Solang Valley and the high-altitude Rohtang Pass. The town has a mix of natural beauty and cultural heritage, with attractions like Hadimba Temple, Manu Temple, and vibrant local markets.
